BSC Applications

Get More done with Less Clicks

BSC Applications brings side pane navigation. With this you can navigate from task to task faster.

Notes

Rich Text, Save and Edit Rich Text Files(RTF) with ease.

Plain Text, Yes! You can save your rich text document as Plain Text. BSC Applications will convert any formated text to Plain Text and save it. You can also open Plain Text.

Images, Copy and Paste images into your notes to add a bit of flare. Your image will remain in the note even if the source file is deleted.

Spell Check, Don't worry about spelling every word correctly. Notes will check your spelling as you type and offer the correct spelling when right clicked.

Phone Book

Phone Numbers, Gone are the days of looking threw your phones contacts to find a phone number, and back are the days of simple phone books...well kinda.

Small Footprint, No need to worry about size of files when saving a Phone Book. BA Phone Book files are kept small by removing unnecessary content(Whitespace, New Lines, ect).

Photo View

Open, Open all of your favorite images with Photo View.

Copy, When a Photo is open in Photo View you can copy it to your clipboard. Once it is copied you can even paste it in Notes to add a bit of character to your documents.

Todo

Tasks, Never forget a task again with Todo. Open, Complete, and Save todo lists.

Small Footprint(Again), Just like Phone Book, Todo saves all of your uncompleted tasks in a clean and compact form.
