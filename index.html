BSC Applications

Github

Three Amazing Apps.

Notes

Quickly jot down information in formatted text. Notes remembers what you typed so when you navigate to another app you can start right were you left off. Notes also spell checks every wird word you type.

Photo View

Take, Copy, and Open Photos with Photo View. Once you've copied a photo you can paste it anywhere.

Todo

Worry less about remembering things and more about getting things done with Todo. Todo helps organize your life with Todo Lists. Save, Open, and Edit Todo Lists on the fly.

Privacy First.
Tracking Never.

Trackers are Weird!

We think trackers are weird. So we don't track anything. When you close BSC Applications everything is deleted exept for your settings.

Open Source

See it for your self. BSC Applications is 100% open source, From the backend to the settings UI. View, Contribute, and Make it Your Own here.

We don't see Anything

Rather you are typing a document or changing some settings, we don't see anything! All data and settings is saved on-device and never reaches it to us.
