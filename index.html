Install

Easy to use.

BSC Applications is built off of simplicity. Things happen when they need to, and get out of the way when the don’t.

Start where you left off.

Temporary Content lets you start right where you left off.

View all of your photos.

Media view lets you view and share all of your favorite photos. Media view automaticly uses your photos libary so you don’t have to do a thing.

Jot down anything.

Notes lets you jot down any form of information into a formatted text interface.

Track down time.

Stopwatch allows you to count the elapsed time in a clean and easy to understand interface.

Plan things out.

Todo lets you plan things out before you do them. Save and Open todo lists with the click of a button.
