Long live the support cycle.

We try to support as many versions as posible. The current support cycle allows for 10 versions. We plan on keeping older versions around as a form of history.

Privacy Matters.

We think privacy is no joke. Everyone deserves privacy and we bake it into BSC Applications. Nothing..not even crash reports are sent to us.

Multi-use to the core.

BSC Applications is made of multiple apps that help you get things gone. Set todo lists, save rich text files, or view photos in BSC Applications.

Meet the apps.

Media View

View and share all of your favorite photos with media view. Media View automatically displays all of your picture library, so you don't have to do a thing.

Notes

Edit, Open and Save rich formatted notes. Whether you need to type an essay or study some notes, notes has you covered.

Stopwatch

Stopwatch lets you count the elapsed time in a clean and simple interface.

Todo

Create tasks in a simple user interface with Todo. Todo helps you plan things out before doing them.
